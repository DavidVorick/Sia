What are Sianotes?
Sianotes are a temporary asset that are convertible to Siastock upon the completion and launch of Sia. Sianotes are available on the Nxt AE, and must be purchased at the market price using Nxt.

Download an Nxt Client: nxtclient.org
Purchase Nxt: bter.com
Sianote Asset ID: 11593659039925686857

What is Siastock?
Sia (as of the current revision) is composed to 2 major cryptocurrencies. The first is Siacoins, and the second is Siastock. Siacoin is the primary currency of Sia, and most transactions on Sia will be using Siacoin. For example, storage space must be rented from Sia using Siacoin. When storage is rented from Sia, the money goes directly to the hosts. A portion of this income is taken as a network fee and distributed among the owners of Siastock. The owners of Siastock will have a regular income of Siacoins proportional to the total amount of Siacoins being directly spent on storage rental.

One Sianote can be directly converted to 0.00039% of host income from renting storage, or 10,000 for 3.9% of all host income from storage.

Where can I get Sianotes?
Approximately 1250 Sianotes have been sold into circulation by the developers. These are all being traded on the Nxt AE. The developers are no longer introducing Sianotes into circulation, so all purchases of Sianotes must be made on the AE at the market price.
